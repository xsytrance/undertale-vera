# Ember — Easter Eggs 🥚

A running list of the hidden secrets. (Spoilers, obviously.) All are pure UI —
they never touch the SaveTruth wall. Most respect the Options → Display **motion**
toggle and the OS reduced-motion setting (the flashy ones fall back to just the
whisper).

| # | Secret | How to trigger | What happens |
|---|--------|----------------|--------------|
| 1 | **file0** | Tap the soul-sigil (top-left ◆) **5×** | Whisper: *"file0 — the save still remembers you."* (darker on a Genocide save) |
| 2 | **Soul-tap ladder** | Keep tapping the sigil: **10×**, **15×** | Escalating whispers (*"…you're still tapping."* → *"okay, you're clearly determined."* / *"stop."* on Genocide) |
| 3 | **DETERMINATION burst** | Tap the sigil **20×** — or the **Konami code** anywhere: `↑ ↑ ↓ ↓ ← → ← → B A` | Gold screen-flash + a rain of 26 ember-gems + a confirm blip + a whisper |
| 4 | **The whole Underground** | In **Sound Test**, hit **All** | Whisper: *"the whole Underground plays as one."* |
| 5 | **A hidden page** | Spam the **Underground · lore ↻** button **7×** fast | A hidden lore line appears + *"a hidden page turns."* |
| 6 | **Spaghetti** 🍝 | Send a chat message containing **"spaghetti"** | 🍝🍅 rains from the sky + *"NYEH HEH HEH! HAVE SOME SPAGHETTI!"* (your message still sends) |

_Kept up to date as new eggs are buried. Last added: **Spaghetti** (#6)._
